Case Study

EduFlow

Digital Transformation for Education

As lead developer and UX strategist, I designed and prototyped a responsive education platform using design systems thinking, explainable AI patterns, and AI-assisted coding workflows.

1. The Design System

EduFlow's interface was structured using an Atomic Design approach to improve consistency, speed, and scalability. Atoms established visual and interaction foundations (type, buttons, fields), while larger patterns composed these into reusable modules that could be deployed across learning workflows.

2. AI Integration

The platform included AI-powered UI patterns that assisted users while preserving autonomy in decision making. Recommendations and content guidance were paired with explainability cues so users could understand why outputs appeared, what data informed them, and how to adjust behavior confidently.

  • AI-Powered UI: Context-aware support integrated directly into the learning and planning experience.
  • Explainable AI: Transparency patterns emphasized confidence, rationale, and trust for both educators and students.

3. Process & Tech

This case study prototype was built with AI-assisted coding to accelerate delivery from concept to implementation. As lead developer, I translated product direction into a functional, responsive experience that demonstrates technical feasibility and interaction quality across screen sizes.
